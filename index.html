Newton's Laws of Motion

This webpage lists all three of Newton's Laws of Motion with descriptions and explanations.

1st Law: Law of Inertia

An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion at a constant velocity unless acted upon by an external force.

2nd Law: F = ma

The acceleration of an object depends on the net force acting upon it and its mass. It is expressed by the formula: F = m * a.

3rd Law: Action and Reaction

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
